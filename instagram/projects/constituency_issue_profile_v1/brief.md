# Constituency issue profile v1

## Purpose

Create a review-only two-slide constituency carousel that summarizes the classified parliamentary issues raised in recorded debate speeches by the constituency's current TDs.

## Slides

1. **Cover** — constituency name, current member count, and classified-speech count.
2. **Issue profile** — top seven classified issue categories rendered with `horizontal_bar_draft_v1` inside `title_text_media_v1`.

## Interpretation boundary

Counts describe classified recorded speeches. They do not measure policy support, policy effectiveness, constituency need, or a TD's complete parliamentary activity.

## Approval boundary

The project, post layout, visual, and metric remain draft. Generated outputs are review artifacts and cannot be published without explicit factual and visual approval.
